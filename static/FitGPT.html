FitGPT

FitGPT is a personalized fitness recommendation system integrating Apple Watch and HealthKit data. It features a React UI, Firebase for storage, ElasticSearch for querying, and GPT-4 with custom prompts to deliver tailored fitness insights, optimizing user health and engagement.

Project Link

Project Overview

FitGPT is an AI-driven fitness recommendation system designed to help users optimize their health and workout routines. By integrating data from Apple Watch and HealthKit, it analyzes workout trends, tracks progress, and provides actionable insights. Powered by advanced AI, including GPT-4 with custom prompt engineering, FitGPT offers personalized fitness recommendations and a holistic approach to health, covering areas like sleep, nutrition, and recovery.

Key Features

  • Workout Data Analysis
    • Integration with Apple Watch and HealthKit for real-time data.
    • Identifies progression trends, fatigue markers, and muscle imbalances.
  • Personalized Goal Alignment
    • Conversational interface for setting and achieving fitness goals.
    • AI-driven contextual recommendations for optimized workout plans.
  • Effort and Progress Monitoring
    • 28-day effort analysis with consistency tracking.
    • Adaptive recommendations based on performance trends.
  • Smart Suggestions
    • Recovery-aware workout planning.
    • Training intensity balancing and exercise variation insights.
  • Holistic Health Integration
    • Sleep and recovery analysis.
    • Nutrition guidance and stress-level adaptations.
    • Injury prevention and personalized health tips.

Technologies Used

API-Gateway Firebase Flask Javascript Lambda OpenSearch React

Use Case

FitGPT empowers users, such as gym enthusiasts and health-conscious individuals, to optimize their fitness routines and overall health. For instance, Alex, a regular gym-goer, wants to improve their workout routine and track health metrics using their Apple Watch. By syncing their Apple Watch and HealthKit data with FitGPT, Alex gains access to a comprehensive analysis of their workout trends, sleep patterns, and recovery metrics. Through FitGPT’s conversational interface, Alex sets specific goals, such as improving endurance or achieving strength gains. FitGPT then uses advanced AI to generate personalized exercise recommendations, monitor consistency over 28 days, and provide adaptive insights to refine Alex’s routine. Additionally, it offers recovery suggestions, stress adaptations, and injury prevention tips, helping Alex achieve their fitness goals effectively and holistically.
